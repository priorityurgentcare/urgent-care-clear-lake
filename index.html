When to Choose Pediatric Urgent Care Instead of the ER

Urgent Care Is Ideal For:

  • Mild fevers
  • Vomiting or stomach cramps
  • Non-severe rashes
  • Cough or sore throat
  • Ear pain
  • Minor cuts or burns
  • Sprains and sports injuries

Go to the ER For:

  • Trouble breathing
  • Head trauma or unconsciousness
  • Seizures
  • High fever with rash in infants
  • Severe allergic reactions
  • Suspected broken bones with deformity
